About me

Hello everyone!
My name is Yulia, I am an ethnic Korean from Ukraine, who is currently studying at the University of Kemon in South Korea. Since 2021 I have experience in teaching Korean and working as a translator for foreign languages clients. I passionate about helping students achieve fluency in language proficiency. My approach to teaching focuses on grammar, vocabulary, and conversational practice, using materials from Seoul National University. I'm trying to create an interesting learning environment where students you can enjoy the beauty of the Korean language and culture. I look forward to sharing my knowledge and experience with you as I guide you to your ways to master Korean!
