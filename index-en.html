About me

Hello everyone!
My name is Julia, I am an ethnic Korean from Ukraine, who is currently studying at the Keimyung University in South Korea. Since 2021 I have experience in teaching Korean and working as an interpreter for foreigners. I passionate about helping students achieve fluency in language proficiency. My approach to teaching focuses on grammar, vocabulary, and conversational practice, using materials from Seoul National University. I'm trying to create an interesting learning environment where students can enjoy and feel the beauty of the Korean language and culture. I am looking forward to sharing my knowledge and experience with you to help you master Korean!
